About Me

Mauricio Tumalán Castillo

Passionate Full Stack Developer | Front End Enthusiast

Hello! I'm Mau Tumalán, a 21-year-old computer science enthusiast born in the vibrant city of Acapulco, Guerrero. Currently, I'm on an exciting journey towards earning my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering at the prestigious Tecnológico de Monterrey University.

My adventure with programming started when I was just 14 years old. Since then, I've been deeply involved in the world of coding, learning, and creating. My primary focus has been on Full Stack development, with a keen interest in Front End development.

As I navigate through the ever-evolving landscape of technology, my eagerness to learn and grow continues to thrive. I'm committed to exploring new horizons in the tech world, constantly seeking to enhance my skills and contribute meaningfully to the field.
